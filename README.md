## Research Question
Does a country's economic profile (GDP per capita, unemployment rate, 
urbanization rate) influence the musical characteristics of its most 
popular Spotify songs?

## Datasets
- Spotify Top 50 Songs by Country (Kaggle)
- GDP per Capita - World Bank (2019)
- Unemployment Rate - World Bank (2019)
- Urbanization Rate - World Bank (2019)

## Key Findings (Milestone 1)
- Higher GDP is associated with lower danceability (r=-0.52, p=0.033)
- Higher unemployment is associated with higher danceability (r=0.60, p=0.011)
- Higher unemployment is associated with higher valence (r=0.51, p=0.035)
